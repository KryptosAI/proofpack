2. Description of Service

ProofPack provides a software-as-a-service platform designed to help AI and digital product companies generate dispute evidence for chargebacks filed through Stripe. The Service includes:

  • Automated generation of a PDF evidence packet when a Stripe dispute is detected.
  • The ability to submit generated evidence packets directly to Stripe on your behalf.
